About GJU

Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology (GJU), Hisar, was established in 1995 by the Government of Haryana. The university offers top-quality education in disciplines like Science, Technology, Management, Pharmacy, and Humanities.

Spread over a sprawling area of about 372 acres, the campus combines natural beauty with modern infrastructure.

History of GJU

Named after the saint‑environmentalist Guru Jambheshwar Ji Maharaj, the university was inaugurated on 1 November 1995.

Over the years, GJU has grown significantly — expanding academic programs, infrastructure, and its student body.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution promoting quality education, research, and innovation.

Our Mission

To develop competent professionals with strong ethical values and leadership skills.
