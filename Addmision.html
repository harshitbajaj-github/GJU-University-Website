Admission Information

Apply for UG, PG, Diploma & PhD Courses

Admission Process

  • Fill online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Appear for entrance test (if applicable)
  • Merit list announcement
  • Counselling & document verification
  • Fee payment and final admission

Eligibility Criteria

  • UG Courses: 12th Pass (Minimum 50%)
  • PG Courses: Graduation (Minimum 50%)
  • PhD: Post Graduation + Entrance Test
  • Diploma: 10th/12th (As per course)

Documents Required

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Graduation Marksheet (for PG)
  • Passport size photo
  • Aadhar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • TC / Migration Certificate

Important Dates

  • Application Start: 01 June 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 30 July 2025
  • Entrance Exam: 10 August 2025
  • Counselling: 20 August 2025
