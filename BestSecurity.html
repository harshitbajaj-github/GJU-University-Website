About University Security

The University Security Department is dedicated to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all students, faculty, and staff. Our trained personnel use advanced surveillance systems and coordinated response strategies to ensure campus safety 24/7.

Campus Security Responsibilities

Patrolling & Security Guards

Trained security personnel patrol the campus to prevent incidents and assist students and staff.

CCTV & Surveillance

Advanced CCTV systems monitor key campus areas for immediate incident response.

Event Security

Special security measures for university events, conferences, and gatherings.

Emergency Response

Quick response to medical emergencies, fire alarms, and safety incidents.

Safety Tips for Students & Staff

Stay Alert

Always be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone in isolated areas.

Emergency Contacts

Keep campus security numbers saved in your phone for quick access.

Report Suspicious Activity

Inform security immediately if you notice any suspicious behavior or unattended items.

Follow Campus Guidelines

Adhere to university safety policies, especially during events and late hours.
